Roman Reigns

Roman Reigns, whose real name is Leati Joseph Anoa'i, was born on May 25, 1985. He is an American professional wrestler and former American football player. Reigns is currently signed to WWE and performs on the SmackDown brand. He is best known for his in-ring persona as "The Big Dog" and is considered one of the most dominant figures in professional wrestling today. Roman Reigns has held the WWE Universal Championship and the WWE Championship multiple times, earning him the title of the "Undisputed WWE Universal Champion." He is also the leader of The Bloodline stable and a proud member of the renowned Anoa'i wrestling family, making him a significant figure in WWE history.
