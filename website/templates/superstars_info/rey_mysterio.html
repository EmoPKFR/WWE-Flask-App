Rey Mysterio

Rey Mysterio, whose real name is Óscar Gutiérrez, is a legendary WWE superstar known for his high-flying acrobatics. He was born on December 11, 1974, and hails from San Diego, California. Mysterio's lucha libre style, characterized by incredible agility and masked persona, has made him an iconic figure in professional wrestling. He is one of the most celebrated cruiserweights in WWE history, having won several championships, including the WWE Cruiserweight Championship. Rey Mysterio's high-energy matches and underdog persona have endeared him to fans worldwide, earning him a place in the hearts of wrestling enthusiasts. Over his illustrious career, Rey Mysterio has competed against some of the biggest names in wrestling and has inspired a new generation of high-flyers.
